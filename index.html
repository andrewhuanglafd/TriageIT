Can Do?

Single-Player Mode

Solo drill against the clock. Pick a deck size — Sprint, Standard, Long, Marathon, or any custom number. Letter grade and rank at the end. Review every miss with the full rationale.

Multiplayer Mode

Host shares a 4-letter code; players join from their phones. Same cards, separate timers. Head-to-head or team-vs-team. Most correct wins; time breaks ties. Top 3 take gold, silver, bronze.

🎓 Classroom Mode

Host a drill without playing. Set deck size, mode, difficulty, and start. You see a live dashboard of every player's progress while they triage. After the round, get a full debrief — leaderboard plus a breakdown of which cards the crew missed most. Tap any flagged card to expand the rationale and review it as a group. All multiplayer features supported (Hard, Expert, head-to-head, team).

⚠ Hard Mode

Borderline RED-vs-BLACK calls only. Apneic patients, agonal breathers, shock-state extremis. Almost no greens. Think before you act.

☠ Expert Mode

Hard Mode deck plus sudden death. Miss one — game over. A randomized line-of-duty incident closes out your run.

Training, Not Protocol

A game cannot replace certified MCI instruction. Real-world triage involves judgment, scope of practice, and incomplete information beyond what cards can capture.
