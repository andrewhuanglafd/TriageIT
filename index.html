The job

Read each patient. Run the START algorithm in order. Pick a tag as fast as you can. Most-correct wins; time is the tiebreaker.

Tag Legend

RED
IMMEDIATE
Reversible life-threat needing rapid care.
RRespirations > 30/min
PPerfusion: no pulse OR cap refill > 2 sec
MMental: cannot follow simple commands
Any one of these = RED
YELLOW
DELAYED
Significant injury, but stable enough to wait.
RRespirations ≤ 30/min
PPerfusion adequate (pulse present, cap refill < 2 sec)
MMental: can follow simple commands
Non-ambulatory but RPM all OK

MINOR
Walking wounded. Direct to a designated area for secondary triage.
✓Patient is walking on their own
RPM is not assessed at this stage

✗No respirations after 1 head-tilt / chin-lift

Multiplayer

Host creates a lobby and shares the 4-letter code. Players join from their phones. Same cards, same order, separate timers. Head-to-head or team-vs-team.

This is training, not protocol

A game cannot replace certified MCI instruction. Real-world triage involves judgment, scope of practice, and incomplete information beyond what cards can capture.
